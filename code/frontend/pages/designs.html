Small Businesses, we're here to help.

With AVCorn, you have the ability to easily swap templates/designs. This page acts an a demo to be able to explore each design on the fly. Our focus and hope with providing so many designs, is that more wide spread small businesses will be able to use our product. No matter the category of the client's small business, we hope we have something to offer to apease to each business category. Scroll below to adventure through what elements you may like for your own website or client's website. Hopefully there's something for everyone!

On this page you can switch over to different designs to see what's possible. Just click one of the design previews, listed below. You can also invoke this same switcher on any page throughout the site, by adding "?design=marketing" to the URL. All you need to do is change "marketing" to the theme you would like to utilize. We have provided you with several different themes to toy around and expriment with, so you can find what's best for you.

On certain devices, you can hover over each preview to see an alternative view of the template. Eacho preview will adapt to the device screen size as well, showing you a previews of what each design will look like in that specific size.

We have a few main foundational designs, along with a list of templates for different small/local businesses. You will find a list of previews for each design below. We always have more business cateogires in development, so if you don't see something that fits you currently, check back soon!
